What's this?

The U.S. government has been sending out Economic Impact Payments through the IRS due to the coronavirus pandemic.

People have reported issues using the IRS status portal. One issue might be address formatting: the tool wants a standard format that not everyone knows, especially for complex addresses like PO boxes or apartments.

What's the form do?

Put in your address. This site will ask the US Postal Service to return a standardized, valid address that matches. This might make it easier to use the portal.

The address information you submit in this form is passed to the United States Postal Service through their Web Tool APIs. Submitted addresses are not logged or stored by this site and are immediately discarded after a result is returned.

This site is not affiliated with the USPS or the IRS. I hope it's helpful, but accuracy isn't guaranteed. Use at your own risk.
